Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 08, 2026  ·  F&F Web Systems

F&F Web Systems ("F&F Web Systems," "Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when individuals visit our website, contact us, use our services, or interact with AI assistant and chatbot features created, hosted, managed, or maintained by F&F Web Systems.

By using our website, submitting information to us, or interacting with an AI assistant or chatbot managed by F&F Web Systems, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

F&F Web Systems may collect information that users voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business address or service area
  • Website and domain information
  • Business hours, services offered, and pricing information
  • Messages submitted through contact forms
  • Chatbot conversations and customer questions submitted through an AI assistant
  • Support requests and information provided during consultations

If you are a customer or website visitor of one of our clients, we may collect information you voluntarily provide through that client's website or AI assistant, including your name, email address, phone number, and chatbot conversation history.

2. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit a website operated, created, hosted, or maintained by F&F Web Systems, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address, browser type, and device type
  • Pages visited, date and time of visit, and referral source
  • General website usage and technical performance information

This information may be used to maintain website functionality, monitor performance, improve user experience, and protect against misuse or unauthorized access.

3. How We Use Information

F&F Web Systems may use collected information to provide website development and AI assistant services, respond to inquiries, capture and forward leads to clients, improve website and chatbot performance, communicate with clients about services and billing, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and improve our internal operations.

4. AI Assistant and Chatbot Data

F&F Web Systems may create and manage AI assistant or chatbot features for client websites. These features may collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, chatbot questions and responses, conversation history, and other information provided during the chat.

Users should not submit sensitive personal information through the AI assistant, including Social Security numbers, banking or credit card information, medical records, passwords, or government identification numbers.

5. Use of OpenAI-Powered AI

F&F Web Systems uses OpenAI-powered artificial intelligence technology to support customer-facing AI assistant and chatbot features. When a user interacts with an AI assistant managed by F&F Web Systems, the user's message may be processed by OpenAI-powered technology to generate a response, answer a question, assist with lead capture, or support customer communication.

AI systems may not always provide accurate, complete, current, or appropriate responses. Users should not rely on AI-generated responses as legal, medical, financial, tax, insurance, emergency, or professional advice.

6. Use of Claude for Internal Website Support

F&F Web Systems may use Claude as an internal tool to assist with website design, planning, layout ideas, writing assistance, troubleshooting, or setup support. Claude is not used to host or operate F&F Web Systems' customer-facing AI assistant unless separately stated.

7. Client Data and Customer Data

When F&F Web Systems provides services to a business client, we may process information on behalf of that client. The client remains responsible for the accuracy of its business information and for maintaining appropriate privacy notices for its own customers and website visitors.

8. How We Share Information

F&F Web Systems does not sell personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary — with the client business that owns the website, with OpenAI-powered systems used to operate AI features, with technical service providers needed to operate our services, when required by law, or to protect against fraud or unauthorized access.

9. Payments and Billing

If payments are accepted through an online payment processor, payment information may be handled by a third-party payment provider. F&F Web Systems does not intentionally store full credit card numbers or complete payment card details.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

F&F Web Systems websites may use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to improve website functionality, understand website traffic, monitor performance, and protect against spam or misuse. Users may disable cookies through browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

11. Data Retention

F&F Web Systems keeps personal information only as long as reasonably necessary — to provide services, maintain client accounts, respond to inquiries, meet legal or business recordkeeping requirements, resolve disputes, and protect against fraud.
